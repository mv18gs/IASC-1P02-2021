Making espresso-like coffee using a moka pot

  1. Put grinds into basket.
    • Fill up evenly to the top.
    • Don't put pressure on grinds (tamp).
    • For best results use freshly ground beans.
  2. Put water into bottom chamber, and screw on top chamber
  3. Put on stove top at medium heat.
  4. Take off stove when you hear loud bubbling - you are done!
